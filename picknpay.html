PICK n PAY

Pick n Pay is a food, grocery, clothing and general merchandise retailer, selling a wide range of products at competitive prices, under our Pick n Pay and Boxer brands.

Our business model describes how the Pick n Pay Group creates long-term, sustainable value for all its stakeholders, through the effective and balanced use of our capitals, and while always keeping the customer at the centre of everything we do. Our business model is underpinned by strong corporate and social governance, with our unique values at its core.
